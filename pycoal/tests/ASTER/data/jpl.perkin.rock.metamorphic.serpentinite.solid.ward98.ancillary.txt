Name:  Verd Antique

Geologic Name:  None

Reference:  Jacobs, E.C., The talc and verd antique deposits of
Vermont: Vermont State Geologist Rept., p. 232-280, 1915-1916.

Microscopic characters:  The rock is a granular aggregate of serpentine
with large areas mainly of talc.  Magnesite may be disseminated
throughout the rock but is usually in veins.  Small grains and 
smears of magnetite occur in the talc areas.
