Name:   Basalt

Geologic Name:  Watchung basalt

Reference:  Lewis, J.V., Petrography of the Neward igneous rocks of New Jersey:
New Jersey Geol. Survey Ann. Report, p. 148-167, 1907.

Microscopic characters:  Hypocrystalline-porphyritic texture.  Lath-shaped
phenocrysts of labradorite are often wholly or completely enclosed in large
clear augite grains.  Small grains of magnetite and leucoxene are abundant.
Chlorite and opaque glass fill interstices.
